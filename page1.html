About Us

It is a traditional house of Rajputs of Jhala clan, which offers a showcase of rural life of traditional state of Rajasthan. An ideal place to explore southern part of Rajasthan to experience rural, tribal and wildlife.

Haveli Gokul Niwas, named after Thakur Gokul Singh Ji of Talawada (1870 - 1930 , built in local gray sandstone of the region. This beautiful blend of Rajasthani and Mewadi architecture reflects the true heritage of Rajasthan. This imposing haveli stands at the nose tip of the village welcoming you.
